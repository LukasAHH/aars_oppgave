Maze escape

Navigate the maze while avoiding traps and collecting key fragments to aid your escape

Player (eleP):
    the playable character, the one you move around and control

Arrow traps (eleAt0/eleAt1/eleAt2/eleAt3):
    Regularly shoots arrows that will kill the player and reselert the game
    need 

Key fragments (eleKf):
    Collecting all of these will change the color of the door and allow you to pass through and win

Golden coins (eleGc):
    Extra collectibles for extra gratification or adding to some score

Empty = 0
Wall = 1
Player = 2
Coin = 3
Fragment = 4
ArrowUp = 5
ArrowLeft = 6
ArrowDown = 7
ArrowRight = 8
