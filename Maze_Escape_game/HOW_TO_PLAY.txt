Maze Escape v0.0.1

** Note ** game is still in extremely early stages of development and has not
** Note ** implemented the "Maze" or "Escape" parts of the game yet

1. Open the game
    Open "run_game.bat" to open the game.

2. Play the game
    Use the arrow keys or the WASD keys to move the character across the board.
    Collect coins (blue triangles) to increase score, coins will respawn after collection.
    The score gained by the coins increases after every coin you have collected.

3. Finish
    There is no ending to the game so it only ends when you close the window or command prompt.
